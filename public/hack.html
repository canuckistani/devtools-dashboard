MetricsGraphics.js is a library optimized for visualizing and laying out time-series data. At just 16KB in size, it provides a simple way to produce common types of charts in a principled, consistent and responsive way. The library currently supports a wide variety of line charts, as demonstrated below. Take a look at the wiki for more. The data here is synthetic and is generated using the included make_fake_data.py script.
